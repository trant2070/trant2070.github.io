My Favorite Foods

My Favorite foods are usually something that consists of noodles. I have grown up with Vietnamese food, one being a Pho, which is one of the most basic things when it comes to Vietnamese food. However, I do like to go outside of just noodles because over the years, I have tried many other different foods from different cultures. Some of which were strange and or very similar to what I am used to. Another one of my favorites is Cơm tấm Thịt Nướng, this is a dish that usually consists of rice, bbq or grilled pork, salad, and fish sauce to pour on the rice and meat.
